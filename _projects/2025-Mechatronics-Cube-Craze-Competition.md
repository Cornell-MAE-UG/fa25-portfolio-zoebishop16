## Project Overview

Worked in a team of three to design, prototype, build, and program an Arduino-based robot for a Mechatronics Cube Craze competition. The goal of the competition was to design a robot that would gather more cubes randomly scattered in a 16 square foot arena than a competitor robot in a one-minute period.
